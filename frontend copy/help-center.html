Getting Started

How do I create an account?

Click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner, fill in your details, and verify your email address. It only takes a few minutes!

What services do you offer?

We offer a wide range of in-home services including beauty & wellness, home repairs, cleaning services, car care, and appliance repair.

How do I find services in my area?

Use our search bar to find services, and we'll show you available providers in your area. You can also browse by category.

Booking Services

How do I book a service?

Search for the service you need, select a provider, choose your preferred time, and complete the booking form. You'll receive a confirmation email.

Can I reschedule my booking?

Yes, you can reschedule your booking up to 2 hours before the scheduled time through your bookings page or by contacting support.

What if I need to cancel?

You can cancel your booking up to 2 hours before the scheduled time. Cancellations made within 2 hours may be subject to a cancellation fee.

Payments & Billing

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and other secure payment methods.

When am I charged?

Payment is processed when you book a service. You'll receive a receipt via email confirming your payment.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and work with trusted payment processors to ensure your financial information is secure.

Account Management

How do I update my profile?

Go to your profile page and click "Edit Profile" to update your personal information, contact details, and preferences.

How do I change my password?

In your profile settings, click "Change Password" and follow the instructions to update your password securely.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can delete your account from your profile settings. Please note that this action is irreversible and will cancel any pending bookings.

Safety & Security

Are your service providers verified?

Yes, all our service providers undergo thorough background checks, identity verification, and skill assessments before joining our platform.

What if I have a safety concern?

If you have any safety concerns, please contact us immediately at woke@gmail.com or call our 24/7 support line.

How do you ensure quality service?

We monitor service quality through customer reviews, ratings, and feedback. Providers must maintain high standards to continue using our platform.

Technical Support

The website isn't loading properly

Try refreshing the page, clearing your browser cache, or using a different browser. If the problem persists, contact our technical support team.

I'm having trouble with the mobile app

Make sure you have the latest version of the app installed. If you're still experiencing issues, try logging out and back in.

I didn't receive my confirmation email

Check your spam folder first. If you still don't see it, try resending the confirmation from your profile settings or contact support.

Still need help?

Our support team is here to help you 24/7
